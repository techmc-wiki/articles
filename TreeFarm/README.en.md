At the invitation of BFladderbean, I have decided to lead the writing of foundational teaching materials and accompanying exercises on tree farms to help everyone understand, use, and design tree farms.

Due to the irreplaceable role of tree farms in basic production for Technical Minecraft Survival, and their representativeness in timing and wiring for Mechanical Redstone, we place them at the beginning of the Mechanical Redstone volume, hoping to bring more inspiration to everyone.

In terms of course structure, we separate the 1×1 section from the 2×2 section to provide more targeted explanations of tree farm design and operating principles. This also includes cutting-edge achievements in tree farms such as 4gt all-tree-species, 8gt large spruce, and 12gt dark oak, along with some challenging wiring exercises. We hope students can gain some inspiration from these and contribute to the development of the next generation of tree farms.

Finally, the editor's abilities are limited. If there are errors or shortcomings, please feel free to correct them. Additionally, we will record related video courses as early as possible. Stay tuned.
