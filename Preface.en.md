Graduate Texts in Minecraft (GTMC) is a community-driven Minecraft technical and redstone tutorial project. GTMC can serve as a guide for beginners getting started, as well as documentation for experienced players to gain deeper understanding of Minecraft's internal mechanisms.

The editorial team is committed to providing clear, accurate, and easy-to-understand content. At the same time, community contributors can also participate in writing and improving this project by submitting Pull Requests.

## Explanatory Notes

Each article in the documentation can be divided into a **Basic section** and an **Advanced section**, targeting beginners and experienced players respectively.

Some content in the **Basic section** may be marked with * in the chapter title due to being relatively rare/uncommon.

For ease of understanding, the **Basic explanation** section may contain some generalized statements that are not absolutely rigorous, but do not affect analysis in typical situations. These statements will be marked with superscript[^1] and further explained in the **Advanced** section or footnotes.

The documentation assumes readers have a **fairly basic** understanding of Minecraft and various redstone components. If you encounter problems during reading, you can consult the Wiki on your own. Similarly, some special and atypical situations are not within the scope of this article; these situations are usually already archived and listed on the Wiki. When a concept has such situations, it will be marked with superscript[^2].

The code used in the documentation is decompiled using `1.20.1-yarn`. Special cases will be marked with superscript[^3].
