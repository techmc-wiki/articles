# #20 Engines

---

> "Understanding these, you have already stepped through the door of flying machines. The knowledge that follows, broadly speaking, is all kinds of engines, all kinds of mounting and extensions, and how to elegantly integrate multiple flying machines together. Keep it up!"
>
> --- T's Slime Tech Tutorial Chapter 1 Section 3

In this chapter, we will expand the definition of engines, no longer limited to the few simple flying machines explained in the previous section. We will deeply analyze the agency of engines, achieving the goal of transforming your machine from mounting functional structures onto engines to functional structures themselves becoming engines.

"Agency" is a word that sounds quite "plain", yet it is most appropriate to describe engines. In my opinion, a structure with agency is called an engine. It can be a small core, or it can be an entire whole. Whether it has agency is the standard for measuring an engine.

Of course, wasting time on definitions is boring. Start your learning, and I wish you a pleasant study.
