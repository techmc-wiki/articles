# #10 Introduction

---

> "A tree that fills a man's embrace grows from a tiny sprout; a nine-story tower rises from a heap of earth; a journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step"
>
> --- Laozi

In this chapter, you'll learn about commonly used mods for Slime Tech design, debugging methods, and other preparatory work. You'll also get an introduction to various Slime Tech components and simple flying machines.

The preparatory work section covers a lot of ground, and the first lesson alone might scare off quite a few people. I hope you can stick with it. Once you get through this stuff, the rest will feel much more natural (just kidding... mostly).

That's just how Slime Tech is. Happy learning!
